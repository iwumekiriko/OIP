</p><p><b>Makeen Alikhan</b> (born 10 October 2001) is a <a href="/wiki/Rugby_union" title="Rugby union">rugby union</a> player who plays for <a href="/wiki/Anthem_Rugby_Carolina" title="Anthem Rugby Carolina">Anthem Rugby Carolina</a> in the <a href="/wiki/MLR_Rugby" class="mw-redirect" title="MLR Rugby">MLR</a>.<sup id="cite&#95;ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>2<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup><sup id="cite&#95;ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>3<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup><sup id="cite&#95;ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>4<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> He also has played for the <a href="/wiki/United_States_national_rugby_union_team" class="mw-redirect" title="United States national rugby union team">United States national rugby union team</a> since 2025.
</p>

<div class="mw-heading mw-heading2"><h2 id="Career">Career</h2><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Makeen_Alikhan&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>
<div class="mw-heading mw-heading3"><h3 id="Club">Club</h3><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Makeen_Alikhan&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Club"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>
<p>Alikhan captained <a href="/wiki/Epsom_College" title="Epsom College">Epsom College</a> 1st XV in the National Schools Champions Trophy,<sup id="cite&#95;ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>5<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> he was later named in the Schools Rugby 2019/20 Dream Team.<sup id="cite&#95;ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>6<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup>
</p><p>He signed his first professional contract with <a href="/wiki/Harlequin_F.C." title="Harlequin F.C.">Harlequins</a> in 2021, making his debut against <a href="/wiki/Saracens_F.C." title="Saracens F.C.">Saracens</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Premiership_Rugby_Cup" title="Premiership Rugby Cup">Premiership Rugby Cup</a>. His first try for Harlequins came in a 7-45 win over <a href="/wiki/Oxford_University_RFC" title="Oxford University RFC">Oxford University</a>.<sup id="cite&#95;ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>7<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> He made his debut for <a href="/wiki/Worthing_RFC" title="Worthing RFC">Worthing</a> in a 17–0 win over <a href="/wiki/Rochford_Hundred_RFC" title="Rochford Hundred RFC">Rochford Hundred</a>.<sup id="cite&#95;ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>8<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> 
</p><p>He was named in the <a href="/wiki/Dallas_Jackals" title="Dallas Jackals">Dallas Jackals</a> roster for the <a href="/wiki/2024_Major_League_Rugby_season" title="2024 Major League Rugby season">2024 Major League Rugby season</a>.<sup id="cite&#95;ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>9<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup><sup id="cite&#95;ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>10<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> His first appearance came in the second round of the 2024 MLR season, coming off the bench in the 48th minute, of a 23-30 loss against <a href="/wiki/San_Diego_Legion" title="San Diego Legion">San Diego Legion</a>.<sup id="cite&#95;ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>11<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> His first start came in a 27-30 loss against the <a href="/wiki/Houston_SaberCats" title="Houston SaberCats">Houston SaberCats</a>, starting at openside flanker he scored a try in the 45' minute.<sup id="cite&#95;ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>12<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup>
</p><p>Ahead of the 2025 MLR season Alikhan joined <a href="/wiki/Anthem_Rugby_Carolina" title="Anthem Rugby Carolina">Anthem RC</a>, making his debut in the first round of the competition scoring a try against <a href="/wiki/NOLA_Gold" class="mw-redirect" title="NOLA Gold">NOLA Gold</a>.<sup id="cite&#95;ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>13<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup><sup id="cite&#95;ref-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>14<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup>
</p>
<div class="mw-heading mw-heading3"><h3 id="International">International</h3><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Makeen_Alikhan&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: International"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>
<p>On 15 May 2020, he was called up the <a href="/wiki/United_States_national_under-20_rugby_union_team" title="United States national under-20 rugby union team">USA under-20</a> squad.<sup id="cite&#95;ref-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-15"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>15<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup><sup id="cite&#95;ref-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-16"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>16<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> He was named in the USA Eagles Extended Player Squad for 2024.<sup id="cite&#95;ref-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-17"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>17<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> He scored a try on his Eagles debut in a win over Belgium in July 2025.<sup id="cite&#95;ref-18" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-18"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>18<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup>
</p>
<div class="mw-heading mw-heading2"><h2 id="Style_of_play">Style of play</h2><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Makeen_Alikhan&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Style of play"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>
<p>An elusive and abrasive player, known for his strong carrying, Alikhan can play across the backrow. Known for his excellent link up play.<sup id="cite&#95;ref-19" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-19"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>19<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup>
</p>
